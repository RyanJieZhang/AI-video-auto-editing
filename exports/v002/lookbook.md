# Lookbook

## Visual Direction

Premium vertical promo preview: dark translucent title blocks, clean white caption blocks, aircraft footage as the hero, blurred full-screen background for platform fit.

## Palette

- Deep aviation navy: `#09182D`
- Sky blue accent: `#79C0FF`
- Clean white: `#FFFFFF`
- Ink text: `#0D1F38`
- Muted metadata: `#485B78`

## Typography

- Chinese title: Microsoft YaHei Bold where available.
- Title size: 58px at 720x1280.
- Caption size: 28px at 720x1280.
- Metadata/tag size: 18-20px.

## Caption System

- Captions sit in the lower third, with enough padding for mobile safe areas.
- One caption per beat.
- Captions should explain the current visual promise, not describe the tooling.

## Card / Graphic System

- Top brand block: dark translucent panel, title and tag.
- Center video: horizontal source framed as a premium inset over blurred background.
- Bottom caption block: white translucent panel with one concise sentence.
- Progress bar: very low visual weight at the bottom.

## Color Grade Notes

- Slight saturation lift.
- Slight contrast lift.
- Mild sharpness lift.
- Avoid crushing dark areas, because aircraft and terminal shots need detail.

## Scene Notes

- If repaired text is visible, keep it inside the framed source video and do not zoom further.
- Use background blur to fill vertical space without pretending the 16:9 source is native vertical footage.
