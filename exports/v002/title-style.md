# Title Style

## Opening Title

Text: `长沙起飞`

Placement: top safe area, inside a dark translucent panel.

Purpose: Make the city and brand context obvious in the first second.

## Captions

Use short Chinese captions that can be read in under two seconds. Do not exceed two lines where possible.

## Callouts

Use tags such as `OPENING`, `SERVICE`, `PROOF`, `POLISH`, and `QA` as small English metadata. These are visual anchors, not explanations.

## End Card

Text: `张府皇家航空 | v002 竖屏预览`

Purpose: Make clear that this is the next iteration, not the final locked commercial version.
