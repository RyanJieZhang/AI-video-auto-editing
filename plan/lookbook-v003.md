# Lookbook v003

## Visual Direction

Cinematic vertical promo. The footage should feel like the hero, with copy layered over gradients rather than large dashboard-style cards.

## Changes From v002

- Less boxed interface styling.
- Larger, darker, more atmospheric vertical background.
- Simple white title over a dark top gradient.
- Compact lower-third caption, not a full explanatory panel.
- Red progress accent to echo the airport `長沙` sign.

## Airport Text Rule

Only the red traditional Chinese `長沙` should remain in the airport sign area. Any older `中国沙`, `中国`, or unrelated red lettering must be removed.

## Publish Readiness

v003 can be treated as a release candidate only if the quality gate passes at 8/10 or above and the airport sign does not draw attention as an edit.
