# Douyin Platform Profile

## Format

- Recommended ratio: 9:16.
- Strong first-second visual hook is critical.

## Hook

- Start with motion, contrast, or a punchy claim.
- Show outcome, conflict, or mistake immediately.
- Avoid slow logo intros.

## Captions

- Very large, high contrast, short phrases.
- Highlight one key phrase per beat.
- Text must remain inside safe areas.

## Pacing

- Frequent cuts and sound hits.
- Keep scenes short unless there is a strong visual reason to hold.
- Use pattern interrupts to avoid flat pacing.

## Packaging

- Title should be direct and curiosity-driven.
- Cover should have one major phrase, not a paragraph.
