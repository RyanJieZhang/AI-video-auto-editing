# Xiaohongshu Platform Profile

## Format

- Recommended ratio: 9:16.
- Keep the opening visually clean and immediately understandable.

## Hook

- Lead with a practical pain point or result.
- Strong patterns: "别再...", "我终于...", "这样做更稳...".
- Avoid long setup.

## Captions

- Large captions, strong hierarchy, safe margins.
- Use short lines and avoid clutter.
- Important terms can be highlighted with color blocks.

## Pacing

- Fast but readable.
- Use frequent visual resets every 2-4 seconds.
- Keep the screen elegant; avoid too many simultaneous labels.

## Packaging

- Cover text should be short and benefit-driven.
- Description can be personal, practical, and slightly conversational.
- Tags should mix topic and use case.
