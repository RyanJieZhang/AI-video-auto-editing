# YouTube Shorts Platform Profile

## Format

- Ratio: 9:16.
- Keep the message globally understandable when possible.

## Hook

- Use a clear question, claim, or visible transformation.
- Avoid relying only on platform-specific context.

## Captions

- Captions should be large and centered enough for mobile.
- Keep text away from bottom UI areas.
- Use consistent style across the whole short.

## Pacing

- Fast, but less visually crowded than Douyin.
- Maintain clear narrative progression.
- Use replayable moments or a strong closing line.

## Packaging

- Title should be searchable and curiosity-friendly.
- Description can be short.
- Thumbnail is less central than long-form YouTube, but still matters for channel pages.
