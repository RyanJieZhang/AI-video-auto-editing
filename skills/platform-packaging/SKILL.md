# Platform Packaging

Prepare the finished video for upload and review.

## Responsibilities

- Final QA checklist.
- Export settings by platform.
- Title, description, timestamps, hashtags, and posting notes.
- Thumbnail or cover frame plan.
- Accessibility notes such as captions and readable text.

## Workflow

1. Read the final brief, script, timeline, and render output if present.
2. Identify target platform: YouTube, Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn, X, course module, or internal review.
3. Check required aspect ratio, duration, title style, safe areas, and caption expectations.
4. Prepare packaging notes in `exports/packaging.md`.
5. If a cover/title asset is missing, route to `motion-fragments` or `asset-gathering` rather than changing the full edit.

## Output

```md
# Packaging

## Export

## Title Options

## Description

## Timestamps

## Thumbnail / Cover

## Hashtags / Tags

## QA Notes
```

## Do Not

- Do not rewrite the whole video at packaging time.
- Do not change the main timeline except for clear export or cover defects.
- Do not invent platform requirements when the target platform is unknown; state assumptions.
